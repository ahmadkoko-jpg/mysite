Who We Are

Crafting tradition
since Our Company Built

AUK Heritage Textile is a trusted name in wool craftsmanship, blending generations of tradition with modern fashion. With 189 skilled employees, we specialize in producing high-quality wool shawls, garments, and coats for men and women.

Our production facilities combine traditional craftsmanship with modern textile technology — ensuring every fabric meets the highest standards of quality, comfort, and style.
